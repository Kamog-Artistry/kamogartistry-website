Project Summary

Client / Project

  • Client: TOOKE – BIRDC
  • Event: International Trade Expo (UMA 2025)
  • Project: Exhibitor Branding & Print
  • Scope: Exhibition visuals, print collateral, booth-ready layouts
  • Timeline: Brief → design → production → event delivery

What We Delivered

  • Exhibition tent branding
  • Banners / backdrops
  • Pull up / tear drop / stand banners
  • Branded stickers and labels
  • Customised table frames, photo frames

Tools and Production

  • Tools: Adobe Illustrator, Photoshop, InDesign, maintop
  • Large format, xerox,
  • Finishing: laminations, aluminium framing

Goal, Scope & Result

Goal

Help TOOKE – BIRDC stand out at UMA 2025 with clear messaging, strong visuals, and professional exhibitor materials that attract visitors.

What We Did

Designed exhibition-ready visuals and print collateral, refined hierarchy for distance readability, and prepared production files for quick event deployment.

Result

A polished exhibitor presence—consistent branding across banners and collateral, improving visibility and visitor engagement during the expo.

Process

1) Event Requirements

Confirmed booth size, placement needs, and key messages to highlight for expo visitors.

2) Design & Proofing

Created banner and collateral layouts, then refined typography and spacing for fast reading at a distance.

3) Production Readiness

Prepared print-ready files with correct sizes, bleed, and finishing notes for fast and accurate production.

4) Delivery for Expo

Delivered the full set of assets for on-site setup—ready for installation and booth operation during the trade expo.
