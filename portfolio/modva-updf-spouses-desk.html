MODVA — UPDF Spouses Desk

Product labels and recognition materials—designed with a clear proofing workflow and produced with clean, production-ready finishing.

Project Summary

Client / Project

  • Client: MODVA
  • Project: UPDF Spouses Desk
  • Scope: Labels + recognition / branded items
  • Location: Mbuya Hill MODVA HQ
  • Timeline: Proofing → production → delivery

What We Delivered

  • Design concepts + mockups for approval
  • Production-ready artwork (print specs + finishing)
  • Vinyl label output + clean trimming
  • Recognition / award-ready items (as required)
  • Apparels

Tools and Materials Used

  • Tools: Adobe Photoshop, Illustrator, InDesign
  • Workflow: proofing, mockups, print-prep, finishing checks
  • Materials: vinyl labels, print stock (as required), laminations / protective finishing

Goal, Scope & Result

Goal

Create clean, premium-looking labels and recognition materials that match the desk’s brand tone and remain readable, durable, and production-ready.

What We Did

Designed multiple label directions, presented mockups for approval, finalized print specs, produced and finished the items, and prepared them for delivery and presentation.

Result

Approved designs and neatly finished outputs with consistent branding—ready for display, gifting, and official desk use.

Process

1) Discovery & Direction

Confirmed label content, sizes, application surfaces, and overall brand look for the desk.

2) Mockups & Proofing

Produced mockups for approval and refined typography, hierarchy, and spacing for clarity.

3) Production & Finishing

Prepared print-ready files, produced labels/materials, and ensured clean trimming and finishing.

4) QC & Handover

Final checks for alignment, readability, and durability before packaging and delivery.
