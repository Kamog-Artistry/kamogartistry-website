Mobile Money Kiosk Branding

MTN/Airtel kiosk branding done two ways: traditional hand painting & writing for longevity, plus modern signage for kiosks incorporating phone & electronics sales and bill-payment services.

Project Summary

Client / Project

  • Client: Mobile Money kiosk operators (MTN / Airtel kiosks)
  • Project: Kiosk Branding (Traditional + Modern)
  • Scope: Hand-painted branding + signage production
  • Applications: Mobile money, phone & electronics, bill payments
  • Timeline: On-site survey → design/proof → production/painting → installation

What We Delivered

  • Traditional painting & hand lettering for long-term durability
  • Light box signage (modern branding for upgraded kiosks)
  • Vinyl stickers (logos, offers, service lists)
  • Banners: PVC and panaflex options
  • One-way vision stickers (window visibility + branding)

Tools and Materials Used

  • Tools: Adobe Illustrator/Photoshop, cutting/printing workflow
  • Traditional: outdoor paints, brushes, surface prep materials
  • Modern: vinyl media, laminations, light box panels, PVC/panaflex banner media

Goal, Scope & Result

Goal

Increase kiosk visibility and trust while clearly listing services (cash-in/out, bill payments, phones & accessories), using solutions that match the operator’s budget and environment.

What We Did

Provided two branding routes: durable hand-painted branding for longevity and low maintenance, plus modern branding (light boxes, vinyl, banners, one-way vision) for kiosks expanding into electronics and bill services.

Result

Branded kiosks with improved visibility, clearer service communication, and stronger customer confidence—built to last outdoors and remain readable.

Process

1) Survey & Service Mapping

Assessed kiosk placement, surfaces, and traffic direction. Confirmed service list (mobile money, electronics, bill payments) for layout hierarchy.

2) Design, Proofing & Routing

Prepared layouts and decided the best route—traditional painting for durability or modern signage for high-impact branding and quick updates.

3) Production / Painting

For traditional: surface preparation, painting, and hand lettering. For modern: print/cut vinyl, produce banners and one-way vision, assemble light boxes.

4) Installation & Finishing

Installed stickers, banners, and light boxes, then checked alignment, readability at distance, and finishing quality for outdoor durability.
