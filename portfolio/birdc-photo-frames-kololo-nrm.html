BIRDC Photo Frames — Kololo NRM Rally (2026)

Large-format photo frames produced for the “Protecting the Gains” final 2026 rally at Kololo—designed for high visibility, photography, and crowd engagement.

Delivered by Kamog Artistry (U) Ltd.

Project Summary

Client / Project

  • Client: BIRDC
  • Event: Kololo NRM Rally (Protecting the Gains — 2026)
  • Project: Photo Frames (Large-format)
  • Use: Event photography, crowd interaction, campaign visibility
  • Scope: Design + production + finishing

What We Delivered

  • Photo frame designs optimized for camera visibility
  • Large-format printing (high contrast and clarity)
  • Mounting and finishing for durability during handling
  • Multiple units for distribution across event points
  • Final deliverables ready for rally deployment

Tools and Materials Used

  • Tools: Adobe Illustrator, Photoshop
  • Print: large-format output with finishing
  • Materials: Foarm board 5mm / reinforcement (as required)

Goal, Scope & Result

Goal

Produce rally-ready photo frames that encourage participation, support messaging, and remain readable in photos and video coverage.

What We Did

Designed and produced large-format frames with clean hierarchy, strong contrast, and durable finishing suited for active crowd environments.

Result

High-impact photo frames used at Kololo for rally engagement—clear in camera shots and strong in physical presence.

Process

1) Brief & Format Planning

Confirmed frame size, key message placement, and photography requirements for visibility at different distances.

2) Design & Proofing

Built layouts with strong contrast and clean typography, then proofed for camera-readability and safe margins.

3) Production & Finishing

Printed large-format visuals and reinforced with mounting/finishing to withstand handling and movement during the rally.

4) Delivery

Delivered ready-to-use frames for the event with a final quality check on color, alignment, and finishing.
