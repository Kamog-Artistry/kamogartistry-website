Project Summary

Client / Project

  • Project: Social Media Designs
  • Client Base: Multiple (selected samples from 100+ clients)
  • Use Cases: Special days, birthdays, events, posters, announcements
  • Platforms: Instagram, Facebook, X, TikTok, LinkedIn, WhatsApp
  • Workflow: Brief → design → revisions → exports

What We Delivered

  • Special-day creatives: New Year, New Month, New Week
  • Birthday flyers for individuals and brands
  • Event flyers: launches, meetings, conferences, shows
  • Academic posters (e.g., PhD Defence at Makerere University)
  • Promo posts: offers, menus, schedules, service announcements

Tools and Output Formats

  • Tools: Adobe Photoshop, Illustrator
  • Sizes: story, square, portrait, landscape (platform-ready)
  • Exports: JPG/PNG + optional editable files (as required)

Goal, Scope & Result

Goal

Help clients communicate quickly and professionally online—using clear hierarchy, strong visuals, and platform-ready formats.

What We Did

Designed a wide range of social posts from short notices to detailed posters, keeping typography readable and layouts consistent across platforms.

Result

A large portfolio of social creatives—fast turnaround, clean finishing, and easy-to-share assets that support engagement and brand presence.

Process

1) Brief & Content

Collected required text, date/time/location, images/logos, platform targets, and any brand references.

2) Design Direction

Created a layout that fits the purpose—celebration, announcement, promo, or academic poster—then refined hierarchy for quick reading.

3) Revisions

Adjusted wording, alignment, imagery, and size variants for different channels (story, feed, WhatsApp share).

4) Final Exports

Delivered high-quality exports optimized for social platforms, with optional print-ready versions when needed.
