Project Summary

Client / Project

  • Client: Abundance Herbal Products
  • Project: Packaging & Brand Collateral
  • Scope: Labels, stickers, packaging, print support
  • Category: Design • Print • Brand
  • Timeline: Brief → proofs → production

What We Delivered

  • Product label and sticker designs (multi-size)
  • Packaging layout system for different variants
  • Print-ready artwork (bleed, safe zones, exports)
  • Brand collateral (flyers / posters as required)
  • Final export pack for production and reprints

Tools and Materials Used

  • Tools: Adobe Illustrator, Photoshop, InDesign
  • Materials: label stock, vinyl stickers, lamination (as required)
  • Workflow: proofing, prepress checks, finishing checks

Goal, Scope & Result

Goal

Create packaging that looks credible on shelves, communicates key info clearly, and supports consistent branding across product variants.

What We Did

Designed label systems and packaging layouts, ensured clean hierarchy and readability, and prepared production-ready files for repeat printing.

Result

A unified packaging identity—clear, professional, and easy to expand to new variants while keeping brand consistency.

Process

1) Product Audit

Confirmed product variants, sizes, container shapes, and the information that must appear clearly on labels.

2) Label System Design

Designed a consistent label framework and refined typography and spacing for quick reading and a premium look.

3) Print Preparation

Prepared production files with accurate dimensions, bleed, color setup, and exports for different print workflows.

4) Production Support

Supported printing and finishing checks and delivered final export packs for future reprints and updates.
