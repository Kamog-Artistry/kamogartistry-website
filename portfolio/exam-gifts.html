Project Summary

Client / Project

  • Project: Exam Gifts
  • Category: Branding • Print • Packaging
  • Scope: Gift design, customization, production, packing
  • Use: Schools, parents, student groups, institutions
  • Timeline: Brief → design → production → delivery

What We Delivered

  • Gift package concept and theme styling
  • Branded items (as required) + personalization
  • Printed inserts: cards, tags, labels, wrap designs
  • Packaging: boxes/bags + finishing
  • Final packed sets ready for distribution

Tools and Materials Used

  • Tools: Adobe Photoshop, Illustrator
  • Print: stickers/labels, cards, tags, wraps
  • Packaging: gift boxes/bags, ribbons, custom labels (as required)

Goal, Scope & Result

Goal

Create motivating, well-presented exam gifts that feel personal, premium, and ready to hand over—without complicated logistics.

What We Did

Designed a consistent package theme, produced custom printed elements (tags, labels, cards), branded items where needed, and assembled final gift sets.

Result

Ready-to-gift exam packages—neat, customized, and memorable, with consistent branding and clean finishing.

Process

1) Package Planning

Confirmed budget, quantity, theme, and personalization details (names, messages, school colors, etc.).

2) Design & Proofing

Designed tags, labels, and cards, then refined layout and typography for a clean gift look.

3) Production

Printed stickers/labels/cards, prepared any branded items, and organized components for consistent assembly.

4) Packing & Delivery

Assembled and packed each gift set, checked finishing quality, then delivered ready for distribution.
