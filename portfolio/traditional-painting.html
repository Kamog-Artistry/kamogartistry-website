Project Summary

Client / Project

  • Service: Traditional Painting & Fine Arts
  • Category: Design • Print • Brand • Fine
  • Scope: Wall branding, murals, custom fine art commissions
  • Location: Uganda
  • Workflow: Surface prep → painting/artwork → finishing

What We Delivered

  • Wall preparation (as a “canvas” for effective branding)
  • Hand-painted signage and lettering on walls
  • Illustrated wall visuals (drawn images / murals)
  • Custom paintings: oil and acrylic works
  • Fine art media: charcoal on paper, pen drawings, textile designs

Tools and Materials Used

  • Surface prep: cleaning, sanding, sealing/priming (where needed)
  • Paint systems: First Dry Paints, Weather Guard, Vinyl Silk (where necessary)
  • Art tools: brushes, rollers, lining tools, charcoal/ink, canvas/paper media

Goal, Scope & Result

Goal

Deliver long-lasting painted branding and fine art that stays visible outdoors/indoors, communicates clearly, and elevates spaces with handcrafted quality.

What We Did

Prepared surfaces for strong paint adhesion, executed hand lettering and illustrative visuals, and produced custom fine art pieces using appropriate media and protective finishing.

Result

Durable wall branding and premium fine art outputs—crafted with attention to composition, readability, and longevity across different environments.

Process

1) Site Review & Concept

Confirmed the space, message, and style. Measured the wall and planned hierarchy (headlines, services, visuals) or fine art concept direction.

2) Surface Preparation

Cleaned and prepared the wall “canvas” for effective branding—sanding and priming/sealing where needed to improve adhesion and durability.

3) Painting / Artwork Execution

Used suitable paint systems (First Dry Paints, Weather Guard, or Vinyl Silk where necessary) and executed hand lettering, murals, and illustrations with clean line work.

4) Finishing & Protection

Final touch-ups, protective finishing (where required), and a quality check for readability, contrast, and long-term performance.
