Project Summary

Client / Project

  • Client: Voltech Engineering
  • Scope: Rebrand + rollout
  • Location: Voltech House - Najjera
  • Timeline: Phased delivery

What We Delivered

  • Logo refresh + visual direction
  • Office branding (walls, glass, wayfinding)
  • Signage systems and safety visuals
  • Vehicle branding
  • Print collateral and branded items

Tools and Materials Used

  • Tools Adobe Photoshop, Illustrator, Indesign, After Effects, Blender, Sketch Up, Large Formats, Vinyl Plotters,Heat Press, DTF Machine, UV Printing, Drills, Nails, Hammers
  • Materials Vinyl, One Way Vision, Plotted Stickers, Panaflex, PVC Banners, Foam Boards, Acrylics, Weather guard & Fast dry pain

Goal, Scope & Result

Problem / Goal

Refresh the brand and implement a professional, consistent look across office and field operations.

What We Did

Defined the visual direction, produced assets, and executed physical branding (print, vinyl, signage, installation).

Result

A cohesive, credible brand appearance that improves recognition and on-site professionalism.

Process

1) Brand Direction

Logo refinement, color system and usage rules.

2) Production

Print, vinyl prep, signage fabrication and finishing.

3) Installation

On-site placement, alignment and QA checks.

4) Handover

Final walkthrough and documentation.
