Project Summary

Client / Project

  • Client: Rokastars Film Productions
  • Project: Creative Production Support
  • Scope: Promotional creative + print-ready artwork
  • Location: Kampala - Uganda
  • Timeline: Concepts → approvals → rollout

What We Delivered

  • Poster and key visual layouts (film promo)
  • Social media campaign assets (sizes + variants)
  • Print-ready artwork for banners / signage (as required)
  • Brand consistency checks across outputs
  • Event branding

Tools and Materials Used

  • Tools: Adobe Photoshop, Illustrator, InDesign
  • Workflow: proofing, format adaptation, prepress checks
  • Materials: posters/banners stock (as required), laminated finishing

Goal, Scope & Result

Goal

Create promotional materials that communicate the film story clearly, look premium, and remain consistent across print and social formats.

What We Did

Designed key visuals and posters, produced social media sizes, prepared production-ready artwork, and supported rollout across required channels.

Result

A cohesive promo pack—clean, high-impact visuals suitable for social, print posters, and event/launch branding materials.

Process

1) Brief & Assets Collection

Confirmed the film promo requirements, collected photography/stills, and aligned on the desired tone and target audience.

2) Key Visual Direction

Developed layout options focusing on hierarchy—title, cast/credits, dates, and call-to-action placements.

3) Formats & Print Prep

Created social size variants and print-ready exports with correct sizing, bleed, and safe areas for different output formats.

4) Rollout Support

Final checks for clarity and consistency, then delivered export packs ready for social posts, posters, and on-ground promotion.
