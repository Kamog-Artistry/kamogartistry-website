Project Summary

Client / Project

  • Client: KIOSA — Kasawo Islamic Old Students Association
  • Project: Association Branding & Print
  • Scope: Identity support, print materials, event visuals
  • Location: Uganda
  • Timeline: Concept → approval → production

What We Delivered

  • Association-branded posters and notices
  • Event banners and print-ready layouts
  • Certificates / documents branding (as required)
  • Social media visuals for announcements
  • Final export packs (print + digital)

Tools and Materials Used

  • Tools: Adobe Photoshop, Illustrator, InDesign
  • Workflow: mockups, approvals, prepress checks
  • Materials: poster stock, banners, laminated finishing (as required)

Goal, Scope & Result

Goal

Support KIOSA’s identity with clear, respectful, and professional visuals that strengthen communication with members and partners.

What We Did

Designed branded layouts and event materials, adapted content for different formats, and prepared production-ready files.

Result

A consistent set of association materials—clear, culturally appropriate, and ready for events, announcements, and documentation.

Process

1) Requirements & Content

Reviewed messaging needs, audience, and event context to guide tone and layout direction.

2) Design & Proofing

Created layout options and refined typography and spacing for clarity and readability.

3) Print Preparation

Prepared print-ready files with correct sizing, bleed, and safe margins.

4) Delivery & Use

Delivered final materials ready for immediate use across meetings, events, and announcements.
