Project Summary

Client / Project

  • Project: SME Branding Showcase
  • Client Base: Multiple SMEs (selected highlights)
  • Focus: Identity, visibility, and sales-ready branding
  • Deliverables: Print + digital branding assets
  • Workflow: Brief → design → production → rollout

What This Showcase Covers

  • Logo design and identity consistency
  • Stationery and office branding
  • Signage: light boxes, banners, vinyl and shop branding
  • Packaging labels and product presentation
  • Digital assets: social post templates and promo creatives

Tools and Production

  • Tools: Adobe Illustrator, Photoshop, InDesign
  • Production: print, vinyl, finishing, installation (as required)
  • Outputs: ready for rollout + repeat printing

Goal, Scope & Result

Goal

Help SMEs look credible, consistent, and easy to recognize—across storefronts, products, and social media.

What We Did

Delivered end-to-end branding: identity direction, print layouts, signage designs, packaging labels, and digital templates—tailored for each business.

Result

A collection of SME branding outcomes that improve visibility and customer trust—designed for practical real-world use.

Process

1) Brief & Brand Direction

Understood the business, audience, and offer—then set direction for typography, color, tone, and key messaging.

2) Design & Consistency

Built core assets (logos, layouts, templates) and ensured consistency across different formats and placements.

3) Production & Finishing

Prepared print-ready outputs, supported production, and applied finishing suited for outdoor and daily-use environments.

4) Rollout

Delivered final files and installed or handed over products ready for use—signage, packaging, stationery, and digital assets.
